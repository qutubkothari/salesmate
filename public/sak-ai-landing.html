What is SAK AI?

SAK AI is an intelligent WhatsApp automation engine designed for businesses in the UAE and India. Unlike basic chatbots, SAK AI connects directly to your inventory to handle complex orders, apply dynamic pricing, and manage stock levels in real-time—eliminating the need for manual data entry or external CRM logins.

Can SAK AI manage my inventory?

Yes, SAK AI manages inventory autonomously by syncing with your stock database in real-time upon every WhatsApp interaction.

When a customer places an order via WhatsApp, SAK AI instantly checks stock availability, reserves the items, and updates your central inventory. It handles variants, low-stock alerts, and even suggests alternatives if an item is sold out.

Does SAK AI support dynamic pricing?

Absolutely. SAK AI applies unique pricing tiers for different customers automatically based on their profile or location.

Whether you have wholesale rates, retail prices, or customer-specific negotiated contracts, the AI identifies the caller's phone number and fetches their specific price list instantly. No manual calculation required.
